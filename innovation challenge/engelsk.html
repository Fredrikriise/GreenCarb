Greencarb
A green hydrocarbon future

Problem

There's a need for drastic changes in today's climate emissions. For at vi skal bo i et klimanøytralt land, må vi først og fremst endre på hvordan vi lever. Det finnes omtrent 3,2 millioner biler i Norge, hvor er med på å skade klimaet. Biler som kjører på bensin og diesel, har et alt for høyt CO2 utslipp, som påvirker ozonlaget og miljøet vårt. Derfor har vi kommet med en løsning for dette problemet, som vi kaller for GreenCarb. Idéen vår påvirker luftrenheten og klimaet på en positiv måte, samt at den gjør gjenbruk av karbon mulig.

Solution

Our idea is an object that collects all the carbon dioxide that the car produce and the CO2 gets split from carbon dioxide to oxygen and carbon. A sort of filter will filter the carbon dioxide, split the carbon and the oxygen where the carbon is stored in a contatiner while the oxygen is transported to the exhaust systems. This is an idea that is realistic to implement on both new and old cars. It is also possible to implement on new cars. This type of technology exists today with larger machines. Aker Solutions is one of many companies in the world that is using this environmentally friendly technology. With help from research and technology that is already there it is realistic that within 3-5 years it wil exist carbon capture that will fit the exhaust system in a car. With these carbon captures we can, with the help of catalysts and small amounts of electric power from the engin, easily split the carbon dioxide in the exhaust system. We will be world-leading in a revolutionary use of green technology.
